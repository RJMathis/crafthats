Crown Brewing
State: Indiana
City: Crown Point
Description: When the Crown Brewing Company opened its doors in 2008 we had one goal in mind, "Craft the Best Beer Period!" In 2010 our Industrial Porter won a Bronze Medal in the World Beer Cup, Brown Porter category. We offer a selection of six to eight of our hand-crafted beers, fresh and on tap year round. In addition to our foundation beers, we brew special seasonal beers throughout the year. So, stop on in and see what's new!
